Our Story

More Than A Court. A Community.

UrbanPlay was built for Dublin. We saw a sport exploding across Europe and asked why Ireland's capital didn't have a dedicated, premium home for it. So we're building one.

Whether you've never picked up a padel racket or you're chasing your first ranking, UrbanPlay is your place. We're designing every detail — the courts, the coaching, the community — with players in mind.
